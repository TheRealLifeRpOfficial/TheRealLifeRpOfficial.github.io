Roleplay Rules

  • 1. Realism in RoleplayEnsure that all actions within the roleplay are realistic.
  • 2. RDM & VDMOnly engage in shooting at individuals or vehicles if you have a valid reason.
  • 3. New Life RuleUpon dying, start as a new character and do not return to your previous scene.
  • 4. Cuff RushingTo cuff someone, you must say "cuffs" to indicate that you are restraining them.
  • 5. Staff MembersAlways comply with instructions from staff members, who have the final say in matters.
  • 6. Roblox & ER:LC TOSAdhere to the Roblox and ER:LC Terms of Service. Violations will be heavily moderated.
  • 7. Auto-JailingDo not click "arrest" while the suspect is in cuffs; you must transport them instead. If the suspect declines the "detain requests" twice, you may jail them.
  • 8. Green ZonesAll civilian spawns are protected in designated green zones, and no shooting or robbing is permitted.
  • 9. Liveries & UniformsWhen wearing a specific uniform, you must use the corresponding vehicle livery.
  • 10. Crash RoleplayAll crashes exceeding 50mph must be roleplayed. Failing to do so will result in strict moderation.
  • 11. Roleplay RequestSome roleplays require prior permission from staff.
  • 12. BountiesBounties are not permitted to avoid complaints about random deaths.
  • 13. Banned Cars & GunsDo not use any banned cars or guns listed below. Non-compliance will result in punishment.
  • 14. Void RoleplayInvalid or interrupted roleplays should be considered void and disregarded.
  • 15. Meta-gamingIt is not allowed to use information obtained outside of the game.
  • 16. BaitingIt is not permitted to deliberately try to attract the attention of police officers and/or KMar members or to keep them from their work without good reason.
  • 17. Crime & Government EmployeesGovernment employees are strictly prohibited from engaging in any illegal activities or possessing illegal items, unless it is necessary for the execution of their job duties. This is subject to the judgment of a staff member. Before beginning their role within the government, employees are required to ensure that they no longer possess any illegal items.

    The following organizations are subject to this rule:
    • Police Force
    • Royal Netherlands Marechaussee
    • Ministry of Justice
    • Government
    If a government employee is caught engaging in illegal activities, in addition to the standard penalty, they will face immediate dismissal from the relevant organization.

    It is prohibited to possess items that originate from a government service, including but not limited to:
    • Service weapons
    Government employees are not allowed to carry their service weapons outside of duty, if applicable. An exception is made for police officers who have undergone specialized training. These officers are permitted to carry their service weapons outside of duty for the purpose of executing their work.
