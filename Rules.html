Algemene Plaatselijke Verordening (APV)

  • 1. Realism in Roleplay

    Make sure all actions in the roleplay are realistic.

  • 2. RDM & VDM

    Only shoot at people or vehicles if you have a valid reason.

  • 3. New Life Rule

    After death, you start as a new character and do not return to your previous scene.

  • 4. Cuff Rushing

    To cuff someone, you must say "cuff" to indicate that you are binding them.

  • 5. Cooperation with Staff

    Always follow the instructions of staff members; they have the final say.

  • 6. Roblox & ER:LC Terms of Service

    Abide by the Roblox and ER:LC Terms of Service. Violations will be strictly moderated.

  • 7. Auto Arresting

    Do not click "arrest" while the suspect is cuffed; you must transport them. If the suspect rejects "request detention" twice, you may jail them.

  • 8. Green Zones

    All civilian spawns are protected in designated green zones, and shooting or robbing is not allowed.

  • 9. Vehicle Colors & Uniforms

    When wearing a specific uniform, you must use the corresponding vehicle color.

  • 10. Crash Roleplay

    All crashes over 50 mph must be roleplayed. Failure to do so will be strictly moderated.

  • 11. Roleplay Requests

    Some roleplays require prior approval from the staff.

  • 12. Bounties

    Bounties are not allowed to avoid complaints about random deaths.

  • 13. Prohibited Vehicles & Weapons

    Do not use prohibited vehicles or weapons listed below. Non-compliance will result in punishment.

  • 14. Invalid Roleplay

    Invalid or interrupted roleplays should be considered void and ignored.

  • 15. Meta-gaming

    Using information obtained outside the game is not allowed.

  • 16. Baiting

    It is not allowed to intentionally attract the attention of police officers and/or military personnel or to keep them from their duties without good reason.

  • 17. Crime & Government Personnel

    Government personnel are strictly prohibited from engaging in illegal activities or possessing illegal items, unless necessary for the performance of their duties. This is subject to staff judgment. Before starting their role within the government, employees are required to ensure they no longer possess any illegal items.

    The following organizations fall under this rule:

    • Police
    • Royal Military Police
    • Ministry of Justice
    • Government
