Welcome to the Koninklijke Marechaussee information page for the RealLifeRP server. Here, you will find details about the different units within the KMar and a comparison of the ranks between the KMar and the police.

Eenheden binnen de KMar

Unit Description
Brigade Regional units responsible for border control and police tasks.
Brigade Speciale Beveiligingsopdrachten (BSB) Special unit for the protection of high-risk persons and objects.
Hoog Risico Beveiliging (HRB) Special unit for the protection of vulnerable infrastructure and persons.
Brigade Training & Opleiding (BTO) Unit responsible for training KMar personnel.
